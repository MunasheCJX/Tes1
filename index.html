Burtone and Coopër formally registered as Burtone Coopër Pvt Ltd is an Accounting, Tax, Secretarial and Advisory firm with interests in investment and IT solutions

Our Services

Accounting, Tax and Advisory

Our accounting, tax and advisory firm aims to provide comprehensive and personalized financial services to small and medium-sized businesses in Zimbabwe. Our services include accounting, bookkeeping, tax preparation, business planning, and financial consulting. Our goal is to help clients make informed decisions about their finances, reduce their tax liabilities, and grow their businesses.

Learn More

Secretarial

Our secretarial function focuses on the Company formations, PRAZ, NSSA and Tax registration and Payroll, Marketing and Branding needs of our clients.

Learn More

IT Solutions

Our IT software development firm Burtonë Solutions is dedicated to delivering high-quality software solutions to businesses looking to streamline their operations, enhance productivity, and foster growth. We focus our efforts on developing custom software solutions to fit the unique needs of our clients, while also offering ongoing support and maintenance services to ensure the success of our partnership.

Learn More

Valuations and Portfolio Management

We also offer valuation and portfolio management services and our firm works with small and medium-sized businesses to provide them with a comprehensive portfolio management plan for their business. We provide valuation services to help businesses understand their worth and work with CEOs and CFOs to build a portfolio and invest their money in a way that generates significant returns.
